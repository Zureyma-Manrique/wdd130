White Water Rafting Co.

Our mission is to provide the most thrilling and safe white water rafting experiences. We believe in adventure, safety, and unforgettable memories for everyone!

Our History

From our humble beginnings in 1998, when a group of river-loving friends decided to share their passion for white water, White Water Rafting Co. has grown to become a benchmark in rafting adventures. What started with a single raft and the dream of exploring every rapid of the Salto River quickly transformed into a company dedicated to offering unforgettable experiences.

Over the years, we've expanded our fleet, trained expert guides, and discovered new routes, while maintaining our commitment to safety, thrill, and respect for nature. We create lifelong memories for every adventurer who joins us.
